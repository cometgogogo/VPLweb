How to search Britannica for Kids

Enter your search term under in the search bar and press GO. You can search Compton's Encyclopaedia, the Elementary Encyclopaedia or the Dictionary for your subject.

Click on your search results to access the article. You can cite the article for a report by clicking Cite This. The links under More Information will allow you to access pictures, articles from magazines, and more!

There are many different subjects under the Browse category. Click on one of these subjects to access more topics under that category. For example, if you click Science, you will be able to access documents about Astronomy, Plants, Technology, etc.

The Student Center on the main page can help you find different subjects, including an Atlas, Biographies, Dictionary, and more. You can also find help on how to write a research paper or an oral presentation here!
