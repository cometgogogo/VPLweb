Gale Power Search

To access Gale Power Search, select Gale Power Search on the main database page and enter your library card number and PIN.

Enter your search term under Browse Subjects to search all of the Gale Databases that Vaughan Public Libraries has access to.

To access a specific publication, click on Browse Publications and select the number or letter your publication begins with. You can limit your search by audience, publication name, country, subject or language.

Use Advanced Search to search multiple terms, or to limit your results by document type, Publication Name, Subject, Date or Lexile Score.

By clicking Change Databases at the top of the screen, you can select which databases Gale Power Search accesses. Use this option to search up to 26 specific Gale databases including CPI.Q for Canadian Periodicals, Junior Edition for materials for students, and the Culinary Arts Collection for recipes!
