How to search Career Cruising

To access Career Cruising, select Career Cruising from the database list and enter your library card number and PIN.

Not sure which career is for you? To select a career based on your interests, click on Career Matchmaker. Enter your name and click Start Now. You will be asked a series of questions, and be given career suggestions based on your answers.

To get information on a career, including education needed, possible saraly and general job descriptions, click on Explore Careers. You can enter a career to search, or narrow your choices alphabetically, by school subject, or by occupation cluster.

To search for post-secondary career information, click on Education. You can learn about colleges and universities that interest you, search for programs that each school offers, and access apprenticeship training information for trades.

To save your career searches, including matchmaker results, you can create a portfolie. Click on portfolio and create an account. You can access your previous searches and saved information at any time.
